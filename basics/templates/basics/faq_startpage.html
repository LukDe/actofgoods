Information about the home page

Act of Goods is a platform that helps people in emergencies or natural disasters. Give away the items you no longer need and trade with others in your area for items that you do! It's simple: just sign up with your email address and location, so that we know where you can help others, as well as where you would be able to pick up items that you need. Everytime you add a new need, you can change your need location as well as profile location. Act of Goods takes confidentiality very seriously and all of your information will be anonymous. In order to make a trade with another user, simply send them a message using our chat system.

The immediate aid button allows you to start helping others immediately without signing up first!

Why do I have to choose a Location and how does it work?

Providing us with the location enables us to figure out where you can best meet the needs of others, and where others could meet your needs. You are always anonymous, and if you move or have items available in a different location, you can change the location for that specific item, as well as the location on your profile. It is possible to have a different location of an item, than stated on your profile.

Please provide us with your exact location by filling in the form.

What can I do with immediate Aid?

The Immediate Aid button enables you to start helping others immediately. In the category button you can find the current needs that people have. The provide us with your location, and a short yet clear headline of your item. Finally, please provide us with a description of the item and click submit.
